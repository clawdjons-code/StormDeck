Operational caveats

  • Observed PPI quicklook PNGs only; not gridded 3D volume rendering.
  • RHI scans are intentionally excluded from this PPI replay index.
  • Per-sweep range, gates, and Nyquist must be read from manifests; do not assume global geometry.
  • Changes between these two sample volumes are not meteorological temporal deltas.
